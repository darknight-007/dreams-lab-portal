DREAMS Laboratory

The Distributed Robotic Exploration and Mapping Systems Laboratory (DREAMS) does research and development of autonomous robotic systems for mapping and sampling in terrestrial, aquatic, and planetary settings. Scholars and staff associated with DREAMS lab are from diverse backgrounds spanning engineering and sciences.

DREAMS lab is affiliated with Arizona State University's School of Earth and Space Exploration, Center for Global Discovery and Conservation Science, and DreamScape Discovery Initiative, and is located at 500L2 at the Walton Center for Planetary Health (WCPH).
