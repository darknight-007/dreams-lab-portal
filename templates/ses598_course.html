SES 598: Robotics and AI in Earth and Space Exploration

This advanced graduate-level course examines the theoretical foundations and practical applications of robotics and artificial intelligence in Earth and space exploration contexts. The curriculum integrates autonomous systems engineering, computer vision algorithms, and machine learning methodologies, with emphasis on their applications in terrestrial and extraterrestrial robotic systems. Through rigorous theoretical study and hands-on experimentation, students will develop expertise in state-of-the-art techniques for robotic perception, navigation, and decision-making.
